What I Can Bring to Your Team

“I first met Ellie when I was a student at Resilient Coders, a bootcamp that trains people of color for software engineer careers in an industry that rarely sees us represented. They had participated in the previous cohort and, despite having started a new engineering role at Wayfair and having to navigate through the new challenges that it presented, they made themself available to support the current cohort.

Ellie's level of dedication was unmatched. On our fourth week, we began to learn javascript and I personally was struggling to make it make sense. I messaged Ellie, and they were quick to schedule a 1:1 to go over the material and complete some practice problems. Whatever expectations I had for that session, Ellie completely blew them out of the water. Despite barely knowing me, Ellie devoted an entire FOUR hours of their evening to me, and while I walked in completely confused and lacking any confidence in the material, I walked out of our meeting energized, excited, and with the accomplishment of being the first student of my cohort to have "white boarded" javascript. Their mentorship continued even after my bootcamp, when I transitioned into my first engineering role at Wayfair.

Ellie is both gifted in their ability to absorb new material and disciplined at developing their skills, but it's their social and emotional intelligence that allows them to 1) meet students where they're at in their own learning journeys, and 2) help to bridge the gap to their destination. It did not surprise me when I learned from Ellie's own cohort members that part of their success in both Resilient Coders and Wayfair's labs program could be attributed to Ellie's leadership.

More recently, I had the pleasure of hiring Ellie to create a website for my business; I realized that outsourcing would make a better use of my time, and I approached them as my first choice. As usual, Ellie did not disappoint. After I communicated my vision, Ellie went above and beyond and did not deliver on my expectations, but instead far surpassed them. What could have been a simple front-end project, Ellie turned into a full-stack web app with features that will significantly reduce the time and effort I will need to maintain my operations. I cannot communicate how immensely grateful for their work, nor how pleased I am with this project. Ellie's ability to anticipate even the unspoken needs of both the business and consumer lended to an exceptional execution of user experience.

I am most confident that Ellie can achieve absolutely anything to which they set their mind. They possess the competencies necessary for any full-stack role: on a team or solo, managing or supporting, coding or instructing. The pleasure would truly be yours to hire them.”
